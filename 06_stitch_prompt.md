# Interlex: задание для Stitch

Ниже готовый промпт для `https://stitch.withgoogle.com/`. Его можно вставлять целиком, либо использовать по частям: сначала для общей концепции, потом отдельно для ключевых страниц.

## Вариант 1. Основной промпт для генерации концепции сайта

Design a premium multilingual B2B website for `Interlex`, a legal and business consulting company helping foreign entrepreneurs and investors launch, buy, and operate businesses in `Kazakhstan` and `Georgia`.

### Business positioning
Interlex is a high-trust advisory firm that provides:
- business registration for foreign founders
- free economic zone and special tax regime advisory
- legal and accounting support
- corporate management services
- turnkey operational setup
- investor support and government relations
- M&A advisory
- due diligence before acquisitions

### Core value proposition
Two jurisdictions, one partner: Kazakhstan + Georgia.
Interlex handles the full cycle from company registration to post-launch support, investor relations, and transaction support.

### Target audience
- foreign investors
- international entrepreneurs
- trading companies entering the EAEU market
- IT startups seeking tax-efficient structures
- holding groups and corporate clients
- M&A buyers looking for assets in Kazakhstan, Georgia, and CIS markets

### Website goals
- generate qualified leads
- communicate trust and premium expertise
- explain complex legal and business services clearly
- support SEO growth
- guide users to consultation requests

### Required pages
- Home
- About
- Kazakhstan
- Georgia
- Business Registration in Kazakhstan
- All Free Economic Zones in Kazakhstan
- Business Registration in Georgia
- All Free Industrial Zones in Georgia
- Virtual Zone / International Company
- M&A
- Due Diligence
- Investors / Government Relations
- Packages & Pricing
- Blog / Knowledge Base
- Contacts

### Visual direction
Create a premium `legal-tech` and `international advisory` look.
The interface should feel:
- credible
- executive
- polished
- modern
- high-trust
- international

Avoid startup-casual aesthetics, generic SaaS gradients, and playful visuals.

### Color palette
- dominant deep navy: `#0F2044`
- premium gold accent: `#C9A84C`
- light neutral background: `#F5F7FA`
- white for contrast sections

### Typography
- elegant serif for headlines, similar in spirit to `Playfair Display` or `Georgia`
- clean sans-serif for body text, similar to `Inter`
- strong hierarchy, spacious layout, restrained luxury

### UI style
- editorial premium hero sections
- structured content blocks
- elegant icon cards
- refined tables and comparison sections
- trust-building sections with metrics, testimonials, team, and partner logos
- clean forms with visible CTA priority
- subtle premium motion, not flashy

### UX requirements
- multilingual-ready layout for `RU`, `EN`, and later `ZH`
- mobile-first responsive design
- sticky header
- clear CTA buttons above the fold
- floating messenger contact button
- breadcrumb-ready inner pages
- FAQ sections on service pages

### Home page sections
- premium hero with headline and dual-jurisdiction message
- services overview
- Kazakhstan vs Georgia comparison
- why Interlex
- key metrics / proof points
- featured services
- packages preview
- testimonials
- partners / trust logos
- final lead form CTA

### Special content modules
- zone directory section for Kazakhstan FEZs
- zone directory section for Georgia FIZs
- tax regime explanation blocks
- package pricing cards
- due diligence process timeline
- M&A asset categories
- investor support workflow

### Tone of design
Confident, premium, international, strategic, and highly professional.
This should look like a firm advising cross-border investors, not a budget agency.

### Deliverable request
Generate a complete website design direction and key screen concepts for desktop and mobile, starting with:
- Home page
- Kazakhstan page
- Georgia page
- Packages page
- M&A page
- Contact page

## Рекомендации по использованию

### Как лучше прогонять в Stitch
1. Сначала вставить `Вариант 1` и получить общую дизайн-концепцию.
2. Затем отдельно прогнать `Вариант 2` для сильной главной.
3. После этого прогнать `Вариант 3` для системы внутренних страниц.
4. Если сервис даёт слишком общий результат, использовать `Вариант 4` и потом уточнять блоками.

### Что уточнять во втором сообщении в Stitch
- make it more premium and less startup-like
- increase legal / executive credibility
- use more editorial structure and less generic SaaS cards
- make Kazakhstan and Georgia comparison more visually strong
- make CTA sections feel more high-value
- improve typography hierarchy
- reduce visual noise

### Чего лучше избегать в промпте
- слишком длинных списков микрофункций
- технических деталей про CMS и стек
- перегрузки SEO-терминами
- смешивания UI-задачи и backend-задачи в одном запросе
